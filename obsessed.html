League of Legends

League has been one of my favourite games for years. I mostly play support and love champions that feel clever, playful, and a little chaotic.

Why Neeko Is The Best Champion

Neeko is easily one of the most fun champions in the game. Her ability to transform into teammates, confuse enemies, and surprise people with her ultimate makes every match feel creative.

She rewards clever plays instead of just raw mechanics, which makes her incredibly satisfying to master. Plus her personality is adorable, chaotic, and wholesome at the same time.
