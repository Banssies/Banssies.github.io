League of Legends

League has had a permanent place in my brain for years. I love the mix of strategy, teamwork, character design, and those tiny moments where one smart play changes everything.

I’m especially drawn to champions with playful energy, tricky mechanics, and a little personality. The ones who feel clever instead of just strong always stand out to me most.

Why Neeko Is the Best Champion

Neeko is one of the most fun champions in the game because she turns every match into a chance to be creative. She is playful, weird, clever, and just chaotic enough to be perfect.

Her shapeshifting makes her feel different from almost everyone else. Winning with Neeko feels less like brute force and more like outsmarting people, which makes her way more satisfying.

Also, her whole vibe is adorable. Strong tomato supremacy.
