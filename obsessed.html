League of Legends

League has been one of my favorite games for years. I love the strategy, teamwork, and those moments where one clever play completely changes a fight.

Champions with personality and trickiness always stand out to me more than simple brute force.

Why Neeko Is The Best Champion

Neeko turns every match into a little mind game. Her shapeshifting lets you confuse enemies, trick people, and set up some incredibly satisfying plays.

She rewards creativity instead of just mechanics, which makes her one of the most fun champions in the game.
