About Me

I’m Nahlla, though I also go by Banssies or Bans. I’m a web development student who loves the space where creativity and code meet.

I’m especially drawn to UI/UX design, soft visual storytelling, and building things that feel personal instead of plain.

My style is a mix of pastel charm, vampire-night energy, and gamer chaos. I like making digital spaces feel cozy, expressive, and a little magical.

What I Enjoy

  • Designing soft, aesthetic interfaces
  • Learning HTML, CSS, and front-end structure
  • Gaming and pulling inspiration from game worlds
  • Creating projects with personality and mood
  • Turning ideas into pretty, usable layouts

Developer Style

I care about how a site feels just as much as how it functions. I want the things I make to be readable, welcoming, and visually memorable.

I’m still growing, but every project teaches me more about design, structure, and how to turn my own style into something real on screen.
